Electronics Department

Overview

The Electronics Department is a dynamic academic unit within an educational institution that focuses on the study and application of electronics. It provides a comprehensive curriculum and practical experiences to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the field of electronics.

The department offers a diverse range of courses covering subjects such as electronic circuits, digital electronics, microprocessors, communication systems, control systems, power electronics, and signal processing. Through these courses, students gain a strong foundation in fundamental principles, theoretical concepts, and practical applications related to electronic systems and devices.

Welcome to the Department Of Electronics And Telecommunication At Aditya Engineering College Beed
.The Department was established in 2001 with the aims to provide the students with a techno-excellent education in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication, considering the ever changing industrial and societal needs.The department runs UG and PG in electronics and telecommunication engineeringElectronics and telecommunication engineering emphasizes on the study of basic electronics as well as the advanced telecommunication field.
