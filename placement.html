PLACEMENT

Overview

Training & Placement (T&P) Cell is an integral part of our institution. The cell functions all through the year to generate placement and training opportunities for students. The cell organizes training activities that prepares students for the campus placement drive. Our campus placement program receives participation from leading corporations, organizations across the country. Our sincere, earnest efforts have borne fruit to form progressive relationships with leading giants in the industrial sector.
Our placement cell works with a high degree of professionalism and is well recognized by corporate and organizations to provide timely information to candidates that fit in the selection criterion.
The cell organizes and coordinates activities collectively with the students and all Head of Departments. The staff coordinators support in all the T&P cell activities. The Placement cell has adopted an effective communication system to keep students informed about potential job opportunities and guides them on application and interview procedures from time to time.
